Band Information

Soundgarden is an alternative and grunge rock band known for their pivotal role in the grunge genre and era in the '90s. The band is comprised of Chris Cornell (vocals), Kim Thayil (guitar), Ben Shepherd (bass), and Matt Cameron (drums), releasing their debut album Ultramega OK in 1988.

Discography

Soundgarden has released many iconic albums throughout their career from 1988-2018. They released six studio albums Ultramega OK, Louder than Love, Badmotorfinger, Superunknown, Down on the Upside, and King Animal. My favorite album is Superunknown, and my favorite songs off this album are Spoonman, 4th of July, and Black Hole Sun.

Notable Albums

Some of their most famous albums include Badmotorfinger, Superunknown, and Down on the Upside.
